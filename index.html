Why I built this.

In college, I was carrying a lot. School, baseball, relationships, and the weight of my own thoughts all at once. If you saw me, you would think I was completely fine. I smiled, I showed up, I competed. But underneath all of that, I was slowly sinking into a place I didn't know how to get out of.

I never really thought about mental health until it found me. I learned what it felt like to wake up heavy, to put on a brave face every single morning, and to wonder why nothing on the other side of any situation ever looked green.

If I could sit across from anyone going through something like that, I'd say this: trust in the plan our Creator has for you. Talk to the people around you. And in the moments where your mind is just too full ‚Äî write it down. Give yourself the space to understand why you think and feel the way you do.

That's where MyBons.ai came from. I started pouring my thoughts into ChatGPT, but it never really felt like mine. So I spent countless hours building something that could. Something personal. Something that helps you discover your own mind, at your own pace.

This is for you. It's built on culture, toughness, strength, and love.
